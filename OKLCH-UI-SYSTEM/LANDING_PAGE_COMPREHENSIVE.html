FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform.

How does the 14-day free trial work?
Start using our Pro plan immediately with full access to all features. No credit card required. After 14 days, you can choose to upgrade or continue with our Free plan.
Can I change plans at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing accordingly.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and for Enterprise plans, we can arrange invoicing and wire transfers.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption (AES-256), SOC 2 Type II compliance, and regular security audits. Your data is backed up daily and stored in multiple geographic locations.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?
Yes! We offer 50% discounts for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact our sales team with your nonprofit or .edu email for details.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
We'll notify you via email when you're approaching your limits. You can upgrade at any time, and we'll never disable your account without warning.
Can I export my data?
Yes! You can export all your data at any time in standard formats (JSON, CSV, HTML). We believe in data portability and never lock you in.
What's your refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked.
Do you provide training and onboarding?
Pro and Enterprise plans include comprehensive onboarding. We offer video tutorials, documentation, live webinars, and for Enterprise customers, dedicated training sessions with your team.
How do I contact support?
You can reach us via live chat (in-app), email (support@example.com), or phone for Pro/Enterprise customers. Our team is available 24/7 with average response time under 5 minutes.
